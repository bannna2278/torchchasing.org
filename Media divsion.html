About the Media Divsion

The Media Division of TORCH Chasing is dedicated to capturing and sharing the thrilling experiences of our storm chasing adventures. Our team is responsible for documenting chase missions through high-quality photography and videography, providing real-time updates during severe weather events, and producing engaging content for our audience. We work closely with other divisions to ensure that the data and footage we collect are accurately represented and effectively communicated to the public. Whether it's through social media, our website, or live broadcasts, the Media Division plays a crucial role in educating and informing the public about severe weather phenomena.
