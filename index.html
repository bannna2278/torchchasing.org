Who we are

We are TORCH chasing, an organization founded in florida in early 2025. We chase severe weather for brodcasting, education purposes, and research purposes.

What we do

From games to real chasing and livestreams here at TORCH we do it all. We play games with our community members on the platform roblox where we play games like twsited, and helicity among other storm chasing games. We also do real storm chasing where we chase real life severe weather and occasionally we livestream what we do out in the real world.

Where to find us

You may sometimes see us out in the field doing stuff or even at a store! But to contact us use the navigation bar to navigate to the 'contact us' page, from there you can choose how you want to contact TORCH.
